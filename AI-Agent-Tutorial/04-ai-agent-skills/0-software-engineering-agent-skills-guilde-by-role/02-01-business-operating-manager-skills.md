# Business Operating Manager Skills

The Business Operating Manager ensures that the organization's daily operations run smoothly, efficiently, and in alignment with strategic goals. They bridge the gap between strategy and execution.

## Role Definition
Focuses on process optimization, resource allocation, performance monitoring, and cross-functional coordination.

## Skills & Tools

### 1. Process Optimization
Improving efficiency and reducing waste.

- **Tool: Value Stream Mapping**
  - *Description*: Visualizing the flow of materials and information required to bring a product to the customer.
  - *Practice*: Identify bottlenecks and non-value-added activities.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create a Value Stream Map for the customer onboarding process."

- **Tool: SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)**
  - *Description*: Detailed, written instructions to achieve uniformity of the performance of a specific function.
  - *Practice*: Document core business processes to ensure consistency.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Draft an SOP for handling customer refunds."

### 2. Performance Management
Tracking and improving organizational performance.

- **Tool: KPI Dashboards**
  - *Description*: Visual displays of key performance indicators (KPIs).
  - *Practice*: Monitor metrics like CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), LTV (Lifetime Value), and Churn Rate.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Design a KPI dashboard for the sales team."

- **Tool: Balanced Scorecard**
  - *Description*: A strategic performance management tool.
  - *Practice*: Measure performance across four perspectives: Financial, Customer, Internal Process, and Learning & Growth.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create a Balanced Scorecard for the operations department."

### 3. Resource Management
Optimizing the use of assets and personnel.

- **Tool: Capacity Planning**
  - *Description*: Determining the production capacity needed to meet changing demands.
  - *Practice*: Forecast staffing needs based on project pipelines.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create a capacity plan for Q4 based on the sales forecast."

- **Tool: Budgeting & Forecasting**
  - *Description*: Planning financial resources.
  - *Practice*: Track actual spend vs. budget and adjust forecasts.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Analyze the variance between the Q2 budget and actuals."

### 4. Change Management
Guiding the organization through transitions.

- **Tool: ADKAR Model**
  - *Description*: A goal-oriented change management model (Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement).
  - *Practice*: Plan communications and training for new system rollouts.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Draft a communication plan for the CRM migration using the ADKAR model."
