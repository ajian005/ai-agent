# Business Owner Skills

The Business Owner (or CEO/Founder) is responsible for the overall strategic direction, financial health, and commercial success of the product or organization.

## Role Definition
Focuses on profitability, market fit, strategic growth, and resource allocation. Prioritizes high-level business goals over technical implementation details.

## Skills & Tools

### 1. Strategic Planning
Defining the long-term vision and ensuring all initiatives align with it.

- **Tool: Business Model Canvas**
  - *Description*: A strategic management template for developing new or documenting existing business models.
  - *Practice*: Identify Value Proposition, Customer Segments, Channels, Customer Relationships, Revenue Streams, Key Resources, Key Activities, Key Partnerships, and Cost Structure.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create a Business Model Canvas for [Product Name]."

- **Tool: SWOT Analysis**
  - *Description*: Framework for identifying Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats.
  - *Practice*: Evaluate internal and external factors affecting the business.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Conduct a SWOT analysis for entering [Market Name]."

- **Tool: OKRs (Objectives and Key Results)**
  - *Description*: Goal-setting framework for defining and tracking objectives and their outcomes.
  - *Practice*: Set quarterly objectives and measurable key results.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Draft OKRs for Q3 focusing on user growth."

### 2. Financial Management
Evaluating the economic viability of projects.

- **Tool: ROI Calculator**
  - *Description*: Method to calculate Return on Investment.
  - *Practice*: Estimate costs vs. expected revenue/savings before approving a project.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Calculate the ROI for implementing feature X given cost Y and expected revenue Z."

- **Tool: P&L Analysis**
  - *Description*: Profit and Loss statement review.
  - *Practice*: Monitor revenue, costs, and expenses to ensure profitability.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Analyze the P&L statement to identify cost-saving opportunities."

### 3. Market Analysis
Understanding the target market and competition.

- **Tool: TAM/SAM/SOM Analysis**
  - *Description*: Estimating Total Addressable Market, Serviceable Available Market, and Serviceable Obtainable Market.
  - *Practice*: Determine the potential market size for a product.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Estimate the TAM, SAM, and SOM for a new AI writing tool."

- **Tool: Competitive Analysis Matrix**
  - *Description*: Comparing features, pricing, and market share against competitors.
  - *Practice*: Identify gaps in the market and areas for differentiation.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create a competitive analysis matrix for top 5 CRM tools."

### 4. Decision Making
Making high-stakes decisions based on data and intuition.

- **Tool: Decision Matrix**
  - *Description*: A chart to evaluate options against a set of criteria.
  - *Practice*: Weight criteria and score options to choose the best path.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create a decision matrix to choose between building in-house vs. buying a solution."

- **Tool: Risk Assessment Matrix**
  - *Description*: Evaluating the likelihood and impact of potential risks.
  - *Practice*: Identify risks and develop mitigation strategies.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Assess the risks of launching in a new region."
