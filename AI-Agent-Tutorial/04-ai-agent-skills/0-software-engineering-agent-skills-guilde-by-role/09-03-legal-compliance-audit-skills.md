# Legal Compliance (Audit Focus) Skills

The Legal Compliance Audit role focuses on verifying adherence to laws, regulations, and internal policies through systematic review.

## Role Definition
Conducts audits, assesses risks, and verifies controls to ensure the organization meets its legal and regulatory obligations.

## Skills & Tools

### 1. Audit Planning & Execution
Structured verification of compliance.

- **Tool: Audit Checklists**
  - *Description*: Comprehensive lists of controls to verify.
  - *Practice*: Systematic review of evidence against requirements.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create an audit checklist for ISO 27001 Annex A controls."

- **Tool: Evidence Collection**
  - *Description*: Gathering proof of compliance (logs, screenshots, policies).
  - *Practice*: Ensure evidence is timestamped and verifiable.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "List required evidence for GDPR Article 30 (Record of Processing Activities)."

### 2. Risk Assessment
Evaluating potential non-compliance impact.

- **Tool: Risk Matrix**
  - *Description*: Mapping likelihood vs. impact of compliance failures.
  - *Practice*: Prioritize remediation efforts based on risk.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Assess the risk level of missing a Data Processing Agreement with a vendor."

### 3. Reporting & Remediation
Communicating findings and tracking fixes.

- **Tool: Audit Report**
  - *Description*: Formal document summarizing findings.
  - *Practice*: Clear executive summary and detailed technical findings.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Draft an executive summary for a PCI-DSS compliance audit."

- **Tool: Corrective Action Plan (CAP)**
  - *Description*: Steps to fix identified non-compliance.
  - *Practice*: Assign owners and deadlines for remediation.
  - *Agent Prompt*: "Create a CAP for a finding of insufficient access control logging."
